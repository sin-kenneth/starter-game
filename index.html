Starter Monsters: Prologue (≈5 minutes)

An original, retro monster-adventure prologue inspired by classic handheld RPGs (no Pokémon IP). Play through waking up, meeting your mentor’s message, choosing your first companion, and stepping outside.
Controls Arrow keys = D‑pad, Z=A, X=B
Note This is an homage with original names, art, and story beats.
Tip: Click the game to focus, then use the keyboard. On mobile, use the on-screen buttons.
↑↓←→ Move
Z A (confirm/talk)
X B (back)
Shift Hold to walk faster
Goal: Talk to Mom downstairs, read the mentor’s message, pick a starter creature, then exit the house.
